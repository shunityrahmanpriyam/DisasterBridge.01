ABOUT US

Mission Statement

At DisasterBridge, our mission is to provide a reliable, digital bridge between people affected by disasters and those ready to help. We aim to make relief faster, more organized, and accessible to everyone in Bangladesh.

Our Organization

DisasterBridge is a newly launched initiative in Bangladesh, created by a passionate team of students, developers, and volunteers. We are building a platform that connects:

  • Victims who need urgent help
  • Volunteers who want to serve on the ground
  • Donors who want to support relief efforts

We believe that with the right tools, communities can respond to disasters more effectively and save more lives.

Our Vision for Impact

Although we are just beginning our journey, our vision is clear:

  • Build a trusted platform for disaster management in Bangladesh.
  • Create a network of volunteers ready to respond anytime.
  • Enable transparent and trackable donations.
  • Ensure every request for help is visible and actionable.

Together, step by step, we aim to grow into a national disaster relief network that makes a real difference.
