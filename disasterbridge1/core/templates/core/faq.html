Help & FAQ

Q1: How do I request aid?
A: Navigate to the Request Aid page, fill in your location, category of aid, urgency, and additional details. Submit, and you'll receive a unique Request ID for tracking.
Q2: Can I donate without creating an account?
A: Yes, donors can make contributions as a guest, but creating an account helps you track donations and receive updates.
Q3: Who can volunteer?
A: Anyone willing to help can sign up as a volunteer. You can choose tasks based on your skills and availability.
Q4: How is my personal information used?
A: We prioritize privacy. Your information is only used to connect you with aid, volunteers, or relevant updates and is not shared with third parties.
Q5: How do I track my aid request or donation?
A: Each request or donation generates a unique ID. Enter it on the platform to see status updates and progress.
Q6: Is there a helpline for urgent cases?
A: Yes, the platform provides emergency contact numbers and live support for high-priority situations.
